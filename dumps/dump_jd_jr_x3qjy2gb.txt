Full Stack Software Engineer
Idea Evolver is seeking an experienced full stack software engineer to join our talented Software as a
Medical Device (SaMD) team working to build first-of-its-kind medical software. You will work with
Vue/React on the front end as well as Golang on the backend to create innovative software on our
Google Cloud Platform.
Details and Qualifications
Proven ability to meet deadlines and deliver solutions quickly at a high quality
Experience developing websites and web applications leveraging popular open source CRMs
Advanced understanding of implementing responsive, highly performant front-end interfaces for
the web
Experience with Google Cloud preferred
Docker/Kubernetes experience
Advanced understanding of one or more of the following: Vue, React, Golang
Experience with automated testing frameworks
Must be able to work as part of a development project from beginning to end
Company Overview
Idea Evolver is an independent Health Marketing, Product Development and FDA Compliance company
specializing in Audience Market Research, Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) and Digital Strategy. We
improve health outcomes through targeted content and application design.
Notable clients include AstraZeneca, Bluebird Bio, United Therapeutics, Novartis and Horizon
Pharmaceuticals.
